# 04 — CONOPS / Use Cases

**Document ID:** TKI-30-66 / DOC-04  
**Version:** 0.4.0  
**Status:** Conceptual

Baseline team: **Gunner** + **Ammo Bearer** — no designator.

---

## Use Case 1: Hasty Ambush Response

**Situation:** Patrol under ISR quadcopter at 100–200 m.  
**Sequence:** Visual ID → gunner seeker lock → fire → BB cloud defeat → reload.  
**Success:** UAS down in one engagement cycle; ≤ 2 rockets.  
**Limitation:** Multiple UAS exceed single team capacity.

---

## Use Case 2: Static LP/OP Defense

**Situation:** 2–6 hour OP; periodic loitering UAS.  
**Sequence:** Cue → lock when in envelope → fire-and-forget → BDA.  
**Success:** OP not confirmed by enemy ISR.  
**Limitation:** Gunner occupied during lock and fire.

---

## Use Case 3: Convoy Escort Dismount

**Situation:** Halted convoy; chase drone 100–300 m.  
**Sequence:** Dismount TKI element; clear backblast; lock; fire; remount.  
**Success:** Defeat before FPV terminal range.  
**Limitation:** Crossing geometry lowers Pk.

---

## Use Case 4: SOF Exfiltration

**Situation:** ISR drone on exfil route.  
**Sequence:** Halt; lock; single F&F shot; resume movement.  
**Success:** Low EM signature vs. heavy EW.  
**Limitation:** Cold-signature drones hard to lock.

---

## Use Case 5: Forward Assembly Area

**Situation:** Platoon FAA; SHORAD elsewhere.  
**Sequence:** Cue → sector gunner engages with TKI.  
**Success:** FAA continues without UAS-triggered fires.  
**Limitation:** Not full FAA air defense replacement.

---

## Use Case 6: Urban Fringe / Compound

**Situation:** Commercial drone ISR over compound.  
**Sequence:** ROE check → lock → fire → assess BB fall area.  
**Success:** UAS defeated with acceptable collateral.  
**Limitation:** **BB downrange hazard** — strict sector and ROE; backblast in enclosed terrain.

---

## Cross-Cutting Notes

| Topic | Guidance |
|-------|----------|
| Lock discipline | Do not fire without seeker ready |
| Ammunition | Guided rockets costly — prioritize high-confidence shots |
| Collateral | Ti BB not HE, but not harmless — brief fall zone |
| Swarm | Engage highest threat; coordinate with EW/small arms |
