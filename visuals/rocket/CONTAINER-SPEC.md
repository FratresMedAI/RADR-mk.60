## Side-view convention (horizontal, left → right)

| End in art | Closure | Gunner action |
|------------|---------|----------------|
| **Left** | **Top** — **pull-tab pop-top** | **PULL** tab; peel/pop off — no tools |
| **Right** | **Breech** — **hand screw cap** | Quick **unscrew** — exposes rocket for inspection — no tools |
| **Body** | Matte OD tube | Stencil **RADR mk.60** (60 mm class) |
| **Right rim** | Aft **shoulder step** | Breech sealing face + retention stop contact (when loaded) |

**KISS:** **Pop top, unscrew bottom** — fast field prep before loading into launcher.

## Top view (cap end)

- Gray **pop-top** disk with red **PULL** tab (matches art).
- Not the screw end — screw cap is on the **breech end** in side profile.

---

## Prepare-round sequence (locked)

1. **Pop** top (pull tab).  
2. **Unscrew** bottom screw cap.  
3. **Inspect** seeker dome and round condition.  
4. Re-seal or proceed to load per SOP (tube slides into bore as one unit; both closures off before insert).
