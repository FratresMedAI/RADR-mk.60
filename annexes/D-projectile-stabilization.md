## Configuration

- **60 mm** smoothbore launch (baseline)  
- **4 swept spring-loaded fins** at **base** — deploy on exit  
- **Small movable canards** at **nose** — low-maneuver trim only  
- **CoG slightly rear-biased** for carry and launch stability  
- Integrated rocket body — **no sabot discard**

Spin-stabilized penetrator architecture is **not** applicable.

---

## Fin & Canard Roles

| Surface | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Fins (4)** | Stability; damping; minimal drag penalty when swept |
| **Canards (nose)** | Small corrections after lock — not high divert |

---

## Flight Phases

| Phase | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Launch | Backblast ≤ **10 yd (30 ft)**; fins spring-deploy |
| Boost 0–2 s | **Lower motor thrust** — recoil/backblast control |
| Boost ramp | Thrust increase for range closure |
| Midcourse | IR homing + canard trim toward 1000 m class intercept |
| Terminal ~20 ft | Proximity (timed backup) → forward cube cone |

---

## Warhead / Balance

Cube pack and motor mass drive a **rear-weighted** balance favorable for shoulder launch. Post-dispersal instability is irrelevant — engagement completes at actuation.

---

## Open Engineering

- Smoothbore confirmation vs. minimal rifling for seating — smoothbore preferred  
- Canard hinge sealing in ravioli-can storage environment
